Privacy Policy

SubPilot AI

Effective date: 2026-06-10

Data You Enter

The app lets you enter subscription names, prices, billing cycles, renewal dates, notes, and pasted receipt text. This information is stored locally on your device.

Data Collection

Version 1 does not create user accounts, does not connect to bank accounts, does not use third-party analytics, and does not send your subscription list to a server.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, the app schedules local notifications on your device before renewal dates.

Export

If you export CSV, you choose where to share or save the file through the iOS share sheet.

Deleting Data

You can delete all local subscription data from the Settings screen in the app.

Contact

For privacy or support questions, open a support request at GitHub Issues.
