What the app does

SubPilot AI helps you record subscriptions, review monthly and yearly cost, track upcoming renewals, mark potential waste, parse pasted receipt text, and export CSV.

Data storage

Version 1 stores subscription data locally on your device. It does not create accounts, connect to bank accounts, or send your subscription list to a cloud service.

Reminders

Renewal reminders use local iOS notifications. If reminders do not appear, check iOS Settings and confirm notifications are allowed for SubPilot AI.

Export and reset

Use Settings to export CSV through the iOS share sheet or delete all local subscription data from the device.

Common Questions

Does SubPilot AI provide financial or investment advice?

No. It is a productivity utility for organizing recurring subscription costs.

Does the app connect to my bank?

No. Version 1 uses manual entry and pasted text parsing only.

Can I move my data elsewhere?

Yes. Export CSV from Settings and save or share it using the iOS share sheet.
